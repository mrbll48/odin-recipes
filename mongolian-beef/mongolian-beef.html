Mongolian Beef

Decription

Ingredients

  • 1 tbps avocado oil
  • 2 tsp ground ginger
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 cup low sodium soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 1/2 pounds flank steak
  • 1/4 cup green onions sliced

Steps

For the Sauce:

  1. Heat 1 tbsp avocado oil in a saucepan over medium heat
  2. Add ginger, garlic, red pepper flakes and stir for 1 minute, or until fragrant
  3. Add soy sauce and water. Bring to a boil and simmer until thickened, about 5 minutes
  4. Pour thickened sauce into a bowl and set aside

For the Steak:

  1. Slice flank steak against the grain into 1/4 inch slices
  2. Heat remaining avocado oil over medium heat
  3. Add sliced steak and cook 2-3 minutes, flipping pieces over to cook both sides
  4. Add the sauce to the pan and cook for 2 minutes
  5. Top with green onions and remove from heat
