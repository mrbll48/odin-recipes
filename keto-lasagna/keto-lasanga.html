Keto Lasagna

Decription

Ingredients

  • 1 tbps avocado oil
  • 1 pound ground italian sausage
  • 1/2 pound ground turkey
  • 1 onion diced
  • 6 cloves of garlic
  • 1 14oz can crushed tomatoes
    • <1i>1 6oz can of tomato paste
  • 1 14oz can of tomato sauce
  • 2 1/2 cups low sodium chicken broth
  • 2 tsp italian seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 8 oz mozarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup parmesean cheese

Steps

  1. In a large pot, heat oil over medium heat
  2. Add italian sausage, ground turkey, and onions. Cook for 8 minutes
  3. Add garlic, saute for 1-2 minutes
  4. Add tomato paste, canned comatoes, tomato sauce, chicken broth, italian seasoning, and pepper. Bring soup to a boil then reduce to a simmer. Simme for 20 minutes.
  5. Remove soup from heat and stir in the mozarella and parmesean cheese. Stir until cheese is fully melted
